F.I.G. — Flightline Information Gateway

Privacy Policy — Last updated June 2025

OVERVIEW

F.I.G. is a free offline-first utility app. This policy explains how your data is handled.

DATA COLLECTION

F.I.G. collects no data. Nothing you enter is transmitted to any server. The developer has no access to anything you do in the app.

LOCAL STORAGE

All data (tracker entries, notes, settings) is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. It never leaves your device. You can export or delete it at any time from within the app.

PERMISSIONS

  • INTERNET — Required only to load the app on first install.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Used only to show online/offline status in the app.

No access to location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage is requested.

THIRD PARTIES

No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

CONTACT

Questions? Contact through the Google Play Store listing.

F.I.G. — No accounts. No tracking. All data stays on your device.
